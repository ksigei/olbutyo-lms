About Olbutyo Boys'


Olbutyo Boys' High School is one of the performing schools in Bomet County. It not only performs well in academics but also in co-curricular activities. It is known for performing well in both rugby 7 and rugby 15. In academics, Olbutyo has been able to send more than 50% of the candidates to various universities in the last 3 years. Since the school is situated in Bomet County, the climate is learning friendly. There is also enough water pumped from R. Chepkulo. The school is sponsored by Catholic Church therefore students grow spiritually.
